"不是因为有希望才坚持；而是坚持才会有希望。" "It’s not that you persist because there is hope; it’s that persistence brings hope." -- unknown

This is the quote that I will remember forever and have become my principle towards the things I’d like to achieve. It's not shameful to give up, but when something is important enough to you, do not simply give up. In the end, we don't want to live a life with regrets.

这是我将永远铭记在心并成为我追求目标的原则的引言。放弃并不可耻，但当某件事对你来说很重要时，不要轻易放弃。因为最后，我们都不想过上后悔的人生。

Suddenly, I've realized that I can also use blogging to express a part of my life.
